Scenarios

  1. What outfit should I wear in order to stay warm?
    Visitors like to use this website to determine what clothes are weather appropriate for the given day. They are interested in temeprature hihs and lows and rain/snow probability.
  2. Will today be good weather to go for a run after work or should I go to the gym?
    A lot of people like to stay active and get as much outdoor exercise as possible, but that often requires planning. Our website helps people decide wether today will be a good day for indoor or outdoor exercise.
  3. Will tomorrow be a good day for an outdoor picnic with friends or should we go to a restaurant?
    People love to have picnics with friends, but not every day is ideal for that. The Weather Inn helps visitors determine wether to have a picnic or opt for an indoor gathering.
  4. Should we take a trip this weekend or stay at home?
    Planning weekend activities requires a reliable weather forecast. The Weather inn helps people decide what activities to plan for the upcoming weekend.
  5. Should I expect a lot of Uber clients today? People are more likely to opt for an Uber if it's raining or cold, and prefer walking or biking on a sunny day. The Weather Inn helps Uber drivers get a better idea f what sort of traffic they can expect that day.
