Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy

Atlassian | atlassian.com

Atlassian takes advantage of visual hierarchy on their website. They first grab the user's attention with a bold white heading on a contrasting blue background, guiding the user through a less prominent caption to a yellow button with a call to action.

White Space

Etsy | etsy.com

In my opinion, the Etsy website is a good example of clean, simple design, and good use of white space. Every element has enough padding around it which makes the site not look crowded and gives it an organizedd feel.

Repetition

EdPresso | educative.io

The EdPresso website is a great example of the principle of repetition. It uses a light shade of brown in the logo as well as other visuals, along with coffee-themed graphics, to tie the whole page together and make it look polished.
